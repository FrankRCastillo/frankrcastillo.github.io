I am a student, family man, US Marine, and programming enthusiast. I enjoy keeping up with politics, international relations, technology, economics, and finance. When I can, I write in my blog.

I attend the University of South Florida; I hope to apply to their PhD in economics by 12/2017. In my research, I hope to find ways to mitigate the risk of money laundering for developing countries and financial institutions looking to do business with them. I also hold an MBA from University of South Florida Saint Petersburg. While in the Marines, I earned a BS in Political Science from University of Maryland University College.

Prior to returning to school, I held several positions as a contractor in the areas of Anti-Money Laundering, financial compliance, and information technology.
