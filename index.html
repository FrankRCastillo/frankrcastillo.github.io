About

I'm willing to have my ideas demolished. Bring a proper wrecking ball.

I am a student, family man, US Marine, and programming enthusiast. I enjoy following and writing about topics of global relevance such as politics, international relations, technology, economics, finance, and culture. I currently attend the University of South Florida where I'm completing math and programming pre-requisites towards an application to the university's PhD program in economics; I hope to submit my application by the end of 2017. Through my research, I hope to find ways to mitigate the risk of money laundering and fraud in developing economies. I also hold an MBA from University of South Florida Saint Petersburg. While on active duty in the United States Marine Corps, I earned a BS in Political Science from University of Maryland University College. Professionally, I've held several positions as a contractor in the areas of Anti-Money Laundering, financial compliance, and information technology.
