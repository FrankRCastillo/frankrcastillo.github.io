About

I'm willing to have my ideas demolished. Bring a proper wrecking ball.

I am a student, family man, US Marine, and programming enthusiast. I enjoy keeping up with politics, international relations, technology, economics, and finance. When I can, I write in my blog. I attend the University of South Florida where I'm taking pre-requisites towards an application to their PhD in economics by 12/2017. My hope is to find ways to mitigate the risk of money laundering in developing economies and the financial institutions looking to do business there. I also hold an MBA from University of South Florida Saint Petersburg. While on active duty in the United States Marine COrps, I earned a BS in Political Science from University of Maryland University College. Prior to returning to school, I held several positions as a contractor in the areas of Anti-Money Laundering, financial compliance, and information technology.
